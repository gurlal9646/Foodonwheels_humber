How do I update my account information?



You can update your name, email, phone number, and profile picture in your app:


  1. Tap “Account” on top right of the screen.
  2. Tap your profile icon then “Settings”.
  3. Tap the detail you want to change and enter the updated information.
  4. You’ll be prompted to enter a verification code or current password to confirm your change via the confirmation e-mail sent on the registered & new Email ID.
